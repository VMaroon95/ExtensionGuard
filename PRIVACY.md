# Privacy Policy — ExtensionGuard

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Summary

ExtensionGuard collects **zero data**. Everything runs locally in your browser.

## Data Collection

ExtensionGuard does **not** collect, transmit, or store any data outside your browser. Specifically:

- **No personal data** is collected
- **No browsing data** is accessed or stored
- **No network requests** are made by ExtensionGuard
- **No analytics or tracking** of any kind
- **No cookies** are set by ExtensionGuard

## Data Storage

ExtensionGuard stores scan results (extension names, permissions, and risk grades) in `chrome.storage.local`. This data:

- Never leaves your browser
- Is only accessible to ExtensionGuard
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions Used

- **management** — To read installed extension information and permissions
- **notifications** — To alert you about risky extensions
- **storage** — To save scan results locally
- **alarms** — To schedule daily re-scans
